About Me

I am a <strong>Sheet Metal Worker</strong> turned <select>Front-End Developer</select>, interested in all things computer programming related.

For over a decade I have been working in the field of construction, installing HVAC systems in the commercial sector. My love of being able to take raw materials and transform them into something greater than the sum of its parts is what first attracted me to the field. It is also what has drawn me to coding.

From the very first > Hello World and javaScript(algorithm), I was hooked. Not only do I get to build amazing and beautiful things, but I get to continue using and strengthening my problem solving skills in a different environment.

The level of control and space available for creating in the digital world is virtually boundless and excites me more than I could have imagined. I look forward to seeing where this path will take me, and it will be nice to get to experience working on the receiving end of a well installed HVAC system.
